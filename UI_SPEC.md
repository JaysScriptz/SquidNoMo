# Approved dashboard specification

The Home screen is intentionally locked to the approved visual direction.

## Required layout

- Persistent neon triangle corner button
- Left sidebar with matching triangle identity
- Navigation order: Home, Games, Players, Guards, Detective, Farming, UI, Settings
- Large hero banner with SquidNoMo title, Dashboard subtitle, radar treatment, matching logo, and minimize button
- Feature Stats panel with notice, Fully On, Partially On, Not On cards, distribution bar, and live counts
- Server Stats panel
- NoMo Stats panel
- Large concise warning card
- Support card directly below the warning

## Prohibited regressions

- No small page title on a window border
- No VIP label
- No AI label
- No development-goal panel
- No donor/supporter list
- No claim that the software is safe
- No tiny toggle controls on the Home dashboard
- No different logo for the corner, banner, sidebar, or minimized state
